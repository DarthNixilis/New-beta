========================
CORE SET KEYWORDS
========================

Combo Attack: As you play this, you may commit another card in your Ring with a matching Trait to add its Momentum to this card's Damage.

Cycling: You may discard this card from your hand at any time to draw a card.

Echo: While this card is in your Market, your Market Size is increased by 1.

Enters: An ability that triggers once, immediately after this card successfully enters the Ring.

Finisher: When an opponent attempts to reverse this, they must pay an additional cost: either pay Momentum from their Ring equal to this card's Damage, or discard a second valid reversal.

Follow-Up: This card may be played immediately after a card played. Then if the specified condition is met the card gets a bonus. This will ignore normal priority changing

Focus Attack: As you play this, you may commit another card in your Ring with the same Target to add its Momentum to this card's Damage.

Ongoing: This card's text provides a continuous effect while it is in the Ring.

Permanent: This card is not removed from the Ring during the End of Turn sequence.

Power Attack: As you play this maneuver, you may have it enter the Ring committed to add its own Momentum value to its Damage.

Recovery: This ability will only happen during the Recovery phase. Any other time the card is played or activated it will not do this ability. 

Relentless: You may play this card from your discard pile by paying its Momentum cost. If reversed from anywhere, it is placed on the bottom of your Draw Deck.

Resilient: You may discard this card from your hand before an opponent's maneuver resolves to reduce its damage by this card's Momentum. You may still reverse that card. 

Rope Break: When a card with Rope Break is tucked during a Pin or Submission, it ends. 

Scout: Look at the top card of your Draw Deck. You may place it into your Discard Pile.

Stun: Your opponent must choose and commit one card in their Ring.

Sudden: You may play this card from your Market by paying double its Momentum cost.

Tie-Up: This ability will only happen during the Tie-Up phase. Any other time the card is played or activated it will not do this ability. 

Trigger: An ongoing ability that activates automatically when the listed condition occurs.

Tuck: Place a card on the bottom of its owner's Purchase Deck.

Uses: This card enters the Ring with Use Counters. It has Permanent while it has one or more Use Counters. When all Use Counters are removed, it loses Permanent.


========================
ADVANCED SET KEYWORDS
========================

Attempt: To Attempt a card means to create a Token copy of that card and immediately play it, following all normal timing rules unless otherwise specified.

Commit: To Commit a card means to turn it sideways. A committed card still exists in the Ring and contributes to game state checks, but cannot be committed again to pay costs. Such as buying cards or contributing to Focus Attack. 

Cunning Attack: As you play this, you may commit a non-Maneuver card in your Ring to add its Momentum to this card's Damage.

Flip: Turn a double-sided Faction card face down to use its once-per-game ability.

Hidden: Whenever you can play a card with Hidden you may play it face down, and choose a Maneuver type (Strike, Grapple, Submission). The card is played as a 0C/2/2 of the chosen type that has 'Enters - This gains Permanent this turn'. It can be played from your ring by paying it's cost and turning it face up. While in your ring a face down card is the 0/2/2 with no type or text

Illegal: Damage from this will not be able to reduce an opponent's Stamina below 1.

Pin: “Try to Pin Opponent” initiates a Pin minigame. 

Ready: This ability will only happen during the Ready phase. Any other time the card is played or activated it will not do this ability. 

Retaliate: This card gains the listed bonus if it is played immediately after you were successfully dealt damage by an opponent's maneuver.

Risky X: Reversals to this card are +ND. 

Special: Things that don't fit anywhere else, they will be described by the card itself. 

Staggered: You are considered Staggered when at or below half your starting Stamina. 

Stealth Attack: As you play this, you may commit another card in your Ring with Hidden or that is face down to add its Momentum to this card's Damage.

Stylish Attack: As you play this, you may commit another card in your Ring with a matching Style to add its Momentum to this card's Damage.

Sturdy: This card counts double its Momentum when defending against Pin or Submit attempts in all zones. Also counts as two cards for Submissions when from hand, deck, and discard. 

Submit: “Try to Submit Opponent" begin the Submission minigame.
