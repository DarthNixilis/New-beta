Power Attack:
As you play this maneuver, you may have it enter the Ring Committed. If you do, add this card’s printed Momentum to its Damage.

Focus Attack:
As you play this maneuver, you may Commit another card in your Ring with a matching Target. Add that card’s Momentum to this maneuver’s Damage.

Combo Attack:
As you play this maneuver, you may Commit another card in your Ring that shares a Trait with it. Add that card’s Momentum to this maneuver’s Damage.

Cunning Attack:
As you play this maneuver, you may Commit a non-maneuver card in your Ring. Add that card’s Momentum to this maneuver’s Damage.

Stealth Attack:
As you play this maneuver, you may Commit a face-down card or a card with Hidden. Add that card’s Momentum to this maneuver’s Damage.

Stylish Attack:
As you play this maneuver, you may Commit another card in your Ring that shares its Style symbol. Add that card’s Momentum to this maneuver’s Damage.

Permanent:
This card is not removed from the Ring during the Ring Cleanup step.

Ongoing:
This card’s abilities are continuously active while it remains in the Ring.

Uses:
This card enters the Ring with N Use counters. It has Permanent while it has at least one counter.

Hidden:
This card may be played face-down as a 0 Cost / 2 Damage / 2 Momentum maneuver. It gains Permanent for the current turn.

Turned:
An ability that triggers only when this card is revealed face-up after being face-down in the Ring.

Flipped:
A one-way transformation where this card turns face-down to resolve its ability, then remains in the Ring as a permanent Momentum source.

Staggered:
This ability is active only while your Stamina is 25 or lower.

Sturdy:
This card counts as double its Momentum when defending during Pin or Submission attempts, and as two cards when Tucked during a Submission.

Finisher:
When an opponent attempts to reverse this maneuver, they must either Commit Momentum from their Ring equal to this card’s modified Damage, or discard an additional valid Response.

Pressing:
To reverse this maneuver, an opponent must Commit one card in their Ring in addition to playing a Response.

Risky N:
Any Response used to reverse this maneuver gains +N Damage.

Resilient:
You may discard this card from your hand during the Response window to reduce incoming Damage by its Momentum.

Cycling:
You may discard this card from your hand at any time you have priority to draw one card.

Rope Break:
If this card is Tucked during a Pin or Submission attempt, that attempt ends immediately.

Follow-Up:
You may play this card immediately after your previous card resolves successfully, ignoring normal priority rules.

Enters:
This ability triggers once, immediately after the card successfully enters the Ring.

Relentless:
You may play this card from your Discard Pile by paying its Momentum cost. If reversed, place it on the bottom of your Draw Deck.

Sudden:
You may play this card directly from your Market by paying double its modified purchase cost.

Stun:
When this resolves, your opponent must Commit one uncommitted card in their Ring.

Scout:
Look at the top card of your Draw Deck. You may leave it there or put it into your Discard Pile.

Tuck:
Move the card to the bottom of its owner’s Purchase Deck.

Echo:
While this card is in the Market, your Market size is increased by 1.

Submit:
Initiate the Submission alternate win condition mini-game.

Retaliate:
This card gains its listed bonus if played after an opponent’s successful card.

Capitalize:
This card gains its listed bonus if played after you successfully reverse a maneuver.

Illegal:
This maneuver cannot reduce an opponent’s Stamina to 0.
